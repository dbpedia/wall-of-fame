Welcome to DBpedia's Wall of Fame. Here you can have a look at all Users registered on the DBpedia Databus. Additionally, you can validate your own WebId with the WebId Validator. The Wall of Fame fetches all registered WebIds daily on 8PM (GMT+1:00). You may need to wait one day until your WebId is shown here. If you have registered a WebId but not or only incompletely find it in the Wall of Fame, look in the logs for errors in your WebId. For further information about WebIds check DBpedia's WebId Repository and the DBpedia Forum.
